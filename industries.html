Custom Sector Templates

How We Support Diverse Industries

Every sector has unique tax liabilities, input cost calculations, and audit requirements. We customize our advisory structures accordingly.

IT & Tech Companies

Handling international transaction transfer pricing, Softex filings under STPI/SEZ guidelines, and double taxation avoidance agreements (DTAA).

Key Deliverable: Transfer Pricing audits (Form 3CEB) & export refunds compilation.

Startups & SMEs

Structuring cap tables, valuation reports under Income Tax and FEMA regulations, ESOP pool designs, and DPIIT tax-holiday audits.

Key Deliverable: Certified valuation reports & Section 56(2)(viib) angel-tax compliance.

Manufacturing Plants

Determining raw material cost of production, processing input tax credit (ITC) reconciliations, and executing plant and machinery depreciation schedules.

Key Deliverable: Inventory valuation (AS-2 compliance) & plant audit declarations.

Healthcare & Clinics

Structuring doctor compensation payrolls, managing service tax exemptions under GST, and reviewing equipment lease accounting ledgers.

Key Deliverable: Doctor contract TDS audit systems & healthcare tax return setups.

Retail & E-Commerce

Managing multi-state GST registrations, reconciling payment gateway transaction invoices, and setting up e-commerce TCS compliance checklists.

Key Deliverable: GSTR-8 marketplace reconciliations & multi-state retail tax filings.

Construction & Real Estate

Auditing construction contracts under AS-7 guidelines, RERA project account reviews, and managing GST reverse charge mechanisms (RCM).

Key Deliverable: RERA designated account certificates & construction cost verifications.

Educational Trusts

Assisting trusts, schools, and private academies in securing Section 12AB tax exemptions, auditing employee PF/ESI payrolls, and structuring scholarship schemes.

Key Deliverable: Section 10(23C) education exemption filing & statutory trust audits.

Transport & Logistics

Auditing commercial fleet asset registers, advising on GST e-way bills generation setups, and handling subcontractor TDS schedules.

Key Deliverable: Logistics GST e-way bill compliance audits & fleet fuel tax models.

Hospitality & Restaurants

Reviewing restaurant food beverage inventory controls, checking GST tax slab setups, and auditing franchise fee transactions.

Key Deliverable: Restaurant GST output computations & POS billing compliance verifications.

Real Estate Developers

Assisting developers in checking joint development agreements (JDA), structuring land capital gains taxes, and verifying RERA project files.

Key Deliverable: Capital gains tax exemptions certificates & joint development filings.

NGOs & Social Sectors

Auditing charity expenditure accounts, filing Section 80G/12A registrations, and tracking FCRA foreign donor receipts schedules.

Key Deliverable: Annual Form 10B audit filings & FCRA donor compliance declarations.

Freelancers & Doctors

Filing returns under Section 44ADA presumptive taxation, auditing personal investment structures, and configuring professional tax calculations.

Key Deliverable: Section 44ADA presumptive returns & personal tax structuring sheets.
