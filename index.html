Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions regarding tax deadlines, startup registrations, ROC compliances, and pricing models.

What documents are required to register a Private Limited Company?

To incorporate a Pvt Ltd company, you need PAN card copies, Aadhaar card copies, passport-size photographs of all directors, along with proof of registered office address (Utility bill like electricity/gas bill and a No Objection Certificate from the property owner).

How do you calculate monthly GST return filing charges?

Our GST packages depend on the number of monthly invoice transactions and the state-level registrations you hold. We charge flat monthly plans starting at standard rates, including GSTR-1, GSTR-3B filings, and ITC reconciliation. Check our pricing matrix for details.

Do you provide representations during income tax assessments?

Yes, we provide representation services for notices, scrutiny cases, and rectification filings. We represent your case professionally before the Income Tax Department authorities, preparing legal submissions and reconciliations.

How long does the statutory audit process take?

A standard statutory audit takes between 2 to 4 weeks depending on the volume of ledger accounts, invoice verifications, and physical stock records. We coordinate closely with your in-house accounting team to expedite execution.

Are virtual CFO services cost-effective for a seed-stage startup?

Absolutely. Hiring a full-time experienced CFO is expensive. Our Virtual CFO package grants you access to top-tier financial models, cash burn analysis, compliance calendars, and investor updates at a fraction of the cost, scaling as you raise capitals.
