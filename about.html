Founder's Message

Guiding Businesses Through Complex Compliance Landscapes

"I set up this practice to bridge a crucial gap. Many businesses struggle to access high-quality regulatory compliance, structured taxation, and statutory audit filings. I aim to supply exact tax planning, corporate structuring audits, and strategic virtual CFO models."

CA. Vikram Singhal is a Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) with more than 10 years of post-qualification experience in Corporate Audit, Indirect Tax Litigation, and Cross-border Valuations. He holds a Diploma in Information System Audit (DISA) from ICAI, qualifying him to conduct comprehensive systems and security audits for corporate ERPs.

Firm Development Timeline

DISA Certification
